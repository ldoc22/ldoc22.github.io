VR - Potion Gatherer

Potion Gathering is a virtual Reality - 3 minute timed experience in the life of a wizard.  Using your eyes, you are instructed to find odd and fasinating ingrediatents to create potions.  The goal is to create as many positions as you can in the 3 minutes.

My work:
I did a large amount of the game mechanics, including the collection of items, scoring, and list making, to name a few.  I also had the oppertunity to voice act for this project!

Alt Controller - Cat Petting Simulator

Cat Petting Simulator is a game designed for all but especially those who are unable to have cats.  The controller is a stuffed cat with various sensor pads, speaker, and arduino.  You are prompted to pet or scratch the cat in different locations with different orientations of the cat.  There is a zen mode, which allows you to play around with the controller.  The single player allows you to see if you can complete the most amount of correct prompts in the time given.  The versus mode is for competing against another player, where you pass the controller back and forth for 5 rounds to see who can win the in a best of 5.  

My work: 
I began work with creating the gesturs class for completing different objectives.
I worked primarily on the vesus game mode.  Developing everything from UI to scoring system.
